WHAT ARE WE?

The idea of groceriesnepal.


Nobody likes a long queues and having limited choices to choose from, so to reduce this frustration we as a team developed our website to make it convinient for people to shop their groceries from their home. Our website prioritizes the local retailers, producers and farmers to be a reliable choice for the people who are seeking simplicity and also quality.

WHAT WE ARE TRYING TO ACHIEVE

At groceriesnepal, we are looking forward to providing people with simple but effective way to get quality ingredients, make the search for high-quality items easier and reduce the limitation on the choices of products so that the people can enjoy looking for products and not be annoyed at long queues and lack of choices.
